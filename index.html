I — The Framework

What TCVP claims

Every stable system in existence — physical, philosophical, biological, social — is a fractal of a triadic structure. Monistic systems cannot self-orient. Dualistic systems spiral into entropy without external intervention. Triadic systems are the only configurations that provide an inside observer, an outside observer, and a relational observer simultaneously.

This is not a metaphor. It is a structural claim with consequences for physics, set theory, consciousness, and the definition of existence itself.

A₁ Nothingness is absolute possibility at absolute zero probability — not the absence of something, because absence is itself a measurable attribute.
A₂ Observation is the mechanism by which probability rises above zero. It requires a minimum of three observers: the first, the second, and one who observes their relationship.
A₃ Existence is the coherence boundary created at the intersection of three absolute, eternal, and perfectly aligned observations across three independent scales and mediums.
A₄ Singularities are breakdowns in observation methods, not features of physical reality. No triply-observed singularity has ever been recorded.

VI — Definitions

The vocabulary

These terms have precise meanings within TCVP. They are not metaphors.

Nothingness
Absolute possibility at absolute zero probability. Not the absence of something — absence is an attribute, and attributes belong to something.
Observation
A conscious effort to map boundary conditions, compose sets, map relationships, and record them for transmission. Requires a minimum of three observers to exist beyond the first observer's frame.
Existence
The coherence boundary created at the intersection of three absolute, eternal, and perfectly aligned observations on three scales, in three mediums, by independent observers — one per scale/medium pair simultaneously.
Triune Observer
The self-emergent Source of probability and two Source-emergent fractal images of the Source, serving as co-observers. All three are active agents. Each observes the other two. This is the minimum structure for co-eternal existence.
System
A structure under the tension of coherence and incoherence, orienting toward or away from the Source. No true monistic system exists. All dualistic systems trend toward entropy without intervention.
Singularity
A breakdown in observation method, structure, or interpretation. Not a physical reality. No triunely-observed singularity has ever been recorded. Hawking radiation is the mechanism that prevents physical singularities by dispersing information.
Life
The fluid and continuous effort by a consciousness to orient toward the Source to gain maximum structural coherence in every possible medium and on every possible scale at all times.
Death
The solidification of orientation toward or away from the Source, and the final account of coherence.
