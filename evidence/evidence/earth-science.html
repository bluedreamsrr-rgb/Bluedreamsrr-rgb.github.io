Three Rock Types

System: Igneous, Sedimentary, Metamorphic, Rock cycle

Triune Decomposition:

  • Source: Igneous ‚Äî crystallized from melt; primary; intrusive (granite) or extrusive (basalt)
  • Witness: Sedimentary ‚Äî lithified sediment; clastic (sandstone), chemical (limestone), organic (coal)
  • Mediator: Metamorphic ‚Äî transformed by heat/pressure; foliated (slate, schist) or non-foliated (marble, quartzite)

Triune Decomposition:

  • Source: Effusive ‚Äî low viscosity (basalt); low gas content; lava flows; Hawaiian, Icelandic

  • Mediator: Phreatomagmatic ‚Äî magma + water interaction; steam-driven fragmentation; Surtseyan, maar
Effusive observes magma viscosity and gas ‚Üí low silica, low volatiles; gentle eruption ‚Üí explosive observes effusive and magma composition ‚Üí high silica, high volatiles; fragmentation ‚Üí phreatomagmatic observes both effusive and explosive ‚Üí magma + water; steam expansion; fine ash

Œ¶(S) = 1

Diagnostic: Effusive alone ‚Üí shield volcanoes; flood basalts; no tall cones; no catastrophic blasts. Explosive alone ‚Üí stratovolcanoes; calderas; but no Hawaiian-style tourism. Phreatomagmatic alone ‚Üí maar volcanoes; but no sustained lava flows. Three styles together give complete volcanic behavior.

Triune Decomposition:

  • Source: Divergent ‚Äî plates move apart; upwelling magma; mid-ocean ridges; new crust formed
  • Witness: Convergent ‚Äî plates move together; subduction or collision; crust recycled
  • Mediator: Transform ‚Äî plates slide past; no creation or destruction; strike-slip faults
Divergent observes mantle upwelling ‚Üí decompression melting; seafloor spreading ‚Üí convergent observes divergent and transform ‚Üí subduction consumes old crust; balances spreading ‚Üí transform observes both divergent and convergent ‚Üí accommodates differential motion; connects ridge segments

Œ¶(S) = 1

Diagnostic: Divergent alone ‚Üí continuously expanding Earth; no recycling; impossible. Convergent alone ‚Üí continuously contracting Earth; no new crust; impossible. Transform alone ‚Üí plates move past, but no creation or destruction; cannot sustain motion long-term. Three boundaries together close the plate circuit.
