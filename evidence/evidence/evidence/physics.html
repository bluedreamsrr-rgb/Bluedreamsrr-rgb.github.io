⚛️ Physics

Instances of triune structure in classical, quantum, and relativistic physics.

Noether's First Theorem

System: Continuous global symmetry → Conservation law

Triune Decomposition:

  • Source: Continuous global symmetry — translation, rotation, time shift
  • Witness: Invariant action δS = 0 — system does not detect change
  • Mediator: Noether current / conservation law — ∂_μ j^μ = 0
Symmetry observes action → action observes conservation → conservation observes symmetry

Φ(S) = 1

Diagnostic: 107 years, foundation of modern physics. Removal of any role breaks the theorem.

Triune Decomposition:

  • Source: Riemann curvature tensor — encodes all geometric information
  • Witness: Bianchi identities — ∇[λ R{μν}]ρσ = 0 (first); ∇^λ R_{λμνρ} = 0 (second)
  • Mediator: Contracted Bianchi identity ∇^μ G_{μν} = 0 — automatic conservation
Riemann observes geometry → identities observe Riemann → contracted identity observes all

Φ(S) = 1

Diagnostic: Pure mathematics; independent of interpretation. This is closure before physics.

Maxwell's Equations

System: Gauss's law, Faraday's law, Ampère-Maxwell law, ∇·B = 0

Triune Decomposition:

  • Source: Gauss's law + ∇·B = 0 — divergence equations; fields from sources
  • Witness: Faraday's law — curl of E; change induces circulation
  • Mediator: Ampère-Maxwell law — curl of B; current + displacement current closes loop
Gauss observes charge → Faraday observes changing B → Ampère-Maxwell observes changing E + current

Φ(S) = 1

Diagnostic: Before Maxwell added displacement current, Ampère's law alone was inconsistent with continuity equation. The addition closed the system.

Diagnostic: No measurement problem; Born rule derived from boundary conditions; compatible with relativity.

Triune Decomposition:

  • Source: Primary mass M₁ — larger body (e.g., Sun)
  • Witness: Secondary mass M₂ — orbiting body (e.g., Earth)
  • Mediator: Test mass m at L4/L5 — 60° ahead/behind; feels combined potential; stable equilibrium
M₁ observes M₂ and m → M₂ observes M₁ and m → m observes M₁ and M₂

Diagnostic: Equilateral triangle configuration is stable when M₁/M₂ > 24.96. Thousands of Trojan asteroids.
